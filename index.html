Products

Smart Bin Project

"A cleaner planet starts with a smarter bin."

Our AI-enabled smart wastebin system is designed to revolutionize urban waste management through innovative technology and sustainable solutions.

Smart Bin Features

AI Camera

This public surveillance system detects and reports illegal waste dumping. It identifies the offender's face and automatically transmits the data to law enforcement.

Real Time Monitoring

This Smart Waste Management System utilizes real-time sensor data on bin fill level and weight to automatically generate and display optimized collection routes via our own software dashboard.

Waste Segregation

This three-compartment bin design simplifies waste segregation. It features glowing, highly visible labels to ensure accurate sorting, even in low-light conditions.

Efficient Compacter & Trash Bags

The integrated compactor technology maximizes capacity, offering over 8x the space. A specialized trash bag system ensures hygienic and convenient waste handling.
